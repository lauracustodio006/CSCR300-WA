Volleyball rules

Main rules

  • A match is played best of 5 sets.
  • Each team is allowed a maximum of 3 touches to return the ball over the net.
  • The ball must stay within the court boundaries to be considered in play.
  • A rally is won when the ball touches the opponentâ€™s court or when the opposing team makes a fault.

Rotation Rules

  • Players rotate clockwise each time their team wins the serve.
  • The serving order must be maintained throughout the set.
  • Back-row players cannot attack the ball above the net in front of the attack line.

Libero Rules

  • The libero is a defensive specialist and cannot attack the ball above the height of the net.
  • Liberos cannot serve, block, or attempt to block (in most leagues).
  • Liberos wear a different-colored jersey for identification.
  • They can replace any back-row player without counting as a substitution.
